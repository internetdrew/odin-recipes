Smothered Chicken Chimichangas

Description

These chimichangas are loaded with savory grilled chicken and cheese, then smothered in a cream green chili sauce. They'll satisfy any craving for Mexican food!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Green chili sauce
  • Sour cream
  • Burrito-sized tortilla shells
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Canola or peanut oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Grill that chicken, baby!
  2. Make the salsa.
  3. Make the salsa.Put it all together on the tortilla shell.
  4. Add some cheese inside so it oozes when you bite into it.
  5. Wrap it all together better than they do at Chipotle.
  6. Fry that bad boy in the oil.
  7. Let it cool.
  8. Open mouth and insert chimichanga.
  9. Chew and enjoy!
