Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a fantastic dish made with pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

This is a crowd favorite loved by children and adults, alike!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Instructions

  1. Boil that pasta, baby!
  2. Lay the pasta in the pan.
  3. Layer in your meat, cheese, and sauce.
  4. Add another layer of pasta.
  5. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until your lasagna is as tall as your oven will allow.
